About OpenTrials

The OpenTrials project aims to shine a light on publication bias, a longstanding problem in trials research. The project will aggregate information from a wide variety of existing sources, and aims to provide a comprehensive picture of the data and documents on all trials conducted on medicines and other treatments around the world.

Open Knowledge are working on this project with Ben Goldacre as Chief Investigator. Ben Goldacre is a Senior Clinical Research Fellow in the Centre for Evidence Based Medicine at the University of Oxford.
